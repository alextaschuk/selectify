About

Last year, I began to listen to albums on a regular basis. I would browse the discographies of artists I liked and take album suggestions from friends, and add them to my saved albums list on Spotify. Eventually, I had over 100 albums saved to my list and choosing an album because a challenge because I had so many options to choose from. This Summer, I decided I wanted to teach myself Python and create a project written in Python that could have a real-life application for me, so I decided to create Selectify. {%endblock%}
